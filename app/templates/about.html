DataSight AI

An intelligent dataset analysis engine designed to instantly summarize, visualize, and answer natural language questions about your data.

Application Workflow

1. Upload CSV

Upload any standard tabular CSV dataset. The system securely loads your file into memory and validates its structure instantly.

2. Analyze Dataset

Our Pandas-powered engine immediately extracts total rows, missing values, column types, and highlights preliminary data quality issues.

3. Ask Questions

Use natural language to query your data. The rule-based Question Engine translates phrases like "highest sales" into dataset operations instantly.

4. Generate Charts

Automatically generate beautiful Matplotlib charts tailored to your data's distribution and your specific queries, without writing a single line of Python.
